Kids Summer Clothes
The ideal clothing for summer is loose pants and shirts, skirts, or trousers. The best colours to wear are yellow, white, beige, and other light shade as they do not absorb heat and trap inside them. Light colours also reflect most of the sun rays back into the atmosphere keeping your body light and sweat-free. Kids play around and tend to sweat more, so it’s important to keep them in loose clothes. It helps them in movement and does not stick to their body.
You can find an amazing summer collection for kids at the Decathlon online store now!
